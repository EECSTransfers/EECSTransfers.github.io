EECS Transfers at Berkeley

Who We Are

EECS Transfers at Berkeley aims to build a community and provide resources to better ensure academic success and career readiness for transfer students interested in the field of electrical engineering and computer sciences. We try to create an open and fun environment where anyone with any skill level can join us!

How to Join

Being an electrical engineering and computer sciences (EECS) major is not a requirement! Only junior-standing and senior-standing transfer students at the University of California, Berkeley are eligible for active membership in EECS Transfers at Berkeley.

Joining our organization is easy, just join our Discord! In order access the full Discord server, you will be asked to verify your transfer status to an officer.

Calendar

Our calendar is a great way to stay up to date with our events. We also have a Google Calendar that you can add to your own calendar, you will need to use your Berkeley Google account to access the calendar.

Note: All events will be restricted to active members unless otherwise stated due to limitations.
