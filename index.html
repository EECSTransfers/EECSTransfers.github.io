EECS Transfers at Berkeley

Who We Are

EECS Transfers at Berkeley aims to build a community and provide resources to better ensure academic success and career readiness for transfer students interested in the field of electrical engineering and computer sciences. We try to create an open and fun learning environment where anyone with any skill level can join us!
