EECS Transfers at Berkeley

Who We Are

EECS Transfers at Berkeley aims to build a community and provide resources to better ensure academic success and career readiness for transfer students interested in the fields of EECS & CS. We try to create an open and fun environment where anyone with any skill level can join us!

How to Join

Being an electrical engineering and computer sciences (EECS) or computer science (CS) major is not a requirement! Only junior-standing and senior-standing transfer students at the University of California, Berkeley are eligible for active membership in EECS Transfers at Berkeley.

To join, please contact us through email: contact [AT] studentorg.berkeley [DOT] edu, or through our Discord! In order to be an active member, you will be asked to verify your transfer status to an officer.

Calendar

Our calendar is a great way to stay up to date with our events. We also have a Google Calendar that you can add to your own calendar, you will need to use your Berkeley Google account to access the calendar.

Note: All events will be restricted to active members unless otherwise stated due to limitations.
